Amazon Smile

Amazon Smile is a program that donates a small percentage of sales to the charity of your choice. The Rensselaer Crows Alumni Foundation is an authorized charity for this program.

To use Amazon Smile from a web browser:

  • Visit smile.amazon.com
  • Sign in with the same account you use for Amazon.com
  • Select Rensselaer Crow Alumni Foundation
    • If you are already using it, you will see a ‘Supporting’ section in the header bar – you can click that, and then click Change Charity
  • Start shopping! Every time you shop using smile.amazon.com, RCAF will likely get some small donation.

How to use AmazonSmile using the Amazon app on your mobile phone:

  • Open the Amazon Shopping app
  • Navigate to the main menu (=)
  • Tap on Settings and then select “AmazonSmile”
  • Select Rensselaer Crow Alumni Foundation, then follow the on-screen instructions to turn ON AmazonSmile in the mobile app
  • Start shopping

To the BA, through RPI’s Giving Day Fund

The RPI Giving Day fun is run through the Office of Institute Advancement. This method of donation is tax deductible and can be used as a vector for tax-free use under Required Minimum Distributions (RMD), including for 401K and IRAs. This method is also a great way to donate for those of you with corporate matching opportunities as well.

The fund is available for the Building Association use, for capital improvements, including maintenance.

To donate this way:

  • Visit the Give Now page for the RPI Capital Campaign
  • Choose Fraternities and Sororities
  • Fill out the amount you’d like to donate to the “Alpha Chi Rho Chapter Support Fund” and the rest of the form as required
  • You’re done.
