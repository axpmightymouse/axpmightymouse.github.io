Ways you can donate to the RPI Crows

All donations to the RCAF or the BA via PayPal, check, and the RPI Giving Day Fund are tax deductible and are eligible for corporate matching. Also, the RCAF can accept donations from an IRA as part of a required minimum disbursement (RMD) and the RCAF is qualified to receive donations from Donor Advised Funds.

There are several ways that you can help out our RPI Crows.

Direct Donation to the RCAF via Paypal

Donation via Paypal is easy, secure, and goes directly to supporting the RCAF with its mission.

Simply click on the button below, and you’ll be redirected to Paypal, where you can easily donate via credit card or bank transfer.

Mail a Check to the RCAF

Mail a check payable to “RCAF” to the following address:

RCAF
PO Box 222
Atglen, PA 19310

We recommend this option for larger donations to avoid paying the PayPal processing fee.
