Spring Semester 2026 Updates, Grad Weekend News, and More!

March 22, 2026

With another calendar year over and the Spring semester begun, it’s time to welcome our new undergraduate Brothers, as well as to congratulate our newly elected board members.

Winter is a time of year that represents change; whether it be the change in temperature or a change in Brotherhood, the changes that we here at Alpha Chi Rho Delta Phi are excited about are our newly initiated Brothers (also known as NIBs for short) and our newly elected officers!

No semester at college is an easy one, and while being diligent in their studies, these new Brothers embarked on their journey in fostering connections, learning valuable life skills and responsibilities (such as caring for a house), and persevered to understand through word and deed, the Brotherhood of all men. We welcome our incoming class of initiated Brothers; it is an honor to witness the continued legacy that you and all the current undergraduate Brothers continue to carry on as the long years roll. We look forward to your time here at Crows and are excited to see what kind of impact you all make!

Other changes are in the air, too! This season marks the transition of many of our undergraduate executive committee members. From attending meetings to making sure our Brothers are fed, each and every person elected previously had a vital role in maintaining our operations as a community. Now, it is time for them to make a change in their lives, and embark on a new goal as they have fulfilled their previous ones with flying colors. Fret not, as we have a bunch of new up and coming faces ready to take on the responsibilities that the previous officers are leaving behind, and we are eager to have them step up to the plate! Thank you and congratulations to:

  • Alex Rieppel, President
  • Jonah Park, Secretary
  • Walter Schmitz, Member at Large

Not only were there changes in leadership within our undergraduate community, but within our own Building Association as well! We are excited to announce the newly elected board members, with Sebastian Martinez ‘23 and Michael Meng ‘25 as our new Members at Large, and Mitchell Dubson ‘24 as Secretary. It goes to show even after we walk across that stage and pick up our diploma, that our strong band can ne’er be broken. Along with the new members, we wish to extend a token of gratitude to the Building Administration as a whole for their diligent work in improving processes and continuing to modernize Delta Phi.

Graduates, save the date on your calendars, since our spring grad weekend has been planned for March 27th–29th 2026. Come around to enjoy ’the good ol days’ and be ready for some ice hockey!

Once again, we are ecstatic with all of the change that is happening at the Delta Phi chapter of Alpha Chi Rho, and want to once again say thank you to all of the Brothers of Crows! Wings Up!
