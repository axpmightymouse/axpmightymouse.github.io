Grad Weekend Fall 2021

November 7, 2021

The RCAF and Delta Phi of Alpha Chi Rho Building Association successfully hosted the bi-annual Grad Weekend event on November 7, 2021. Events were hosted both virtually and in-person.

The General Meeting was held from 10:30AM-12:30 PM Sunday morning with 25+ members in attendance. The meeting minutes have been uploaded to the Google Drive.
